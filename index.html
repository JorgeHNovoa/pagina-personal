About

Hi, I'm Jorge Novoa.

Currently I'm pursuing a Master's degree in Engineering Sciences with a specialization in Applied Mathematics at the University of Chile, where I am developing a double-thesis project.

My main academic and research interests lie in Nonlinear Analysis and its applications to Differential Equations, both Ordinary (ODEs) and Partial (PDEs).

At present, my research is centered on the Leray–Schauder topological degree and its applications to differential equations involving variable exponent operators, aiming to advance the understanding of existence and multiplicity results within this framework.
