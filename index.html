Jorge Novoa

About

Hi, I'm Jorge Novoa. Currently I'm pursuing a Master's degree in Engineering Sciences with a specialization in Applied Mathematics at the University of Chile, where I am developing a double-thesis project.

Research Interests

  • Nonlinear Analysis (particularly fixed-point theorems, topological degree theory, and operator theory in function spaces)
  • Ordinary Differential Equations (focusing on existence and multiplicity of solutions, and boundary value problems)
  • Partial Differential Equations (particularly elliptic equations and problems with variable growth conditions)
  • Leray–Schauder Topological Degree (and its applications to existence theory for nonlinear operators)
  • Variable Exponent Operators (including the p(x)-Laplacian and Sobolev spaces with nonstandard growth)
