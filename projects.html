Análisis no Lineal

Universidad de Chile

This document was prepared to help students review the main ideas of Green's functions and the Perron method. The material was developed in my role as a teaching assistant for the course Analisis No Lineal.

Modelos de Evolución

Universidad de Chile

This document provides a comprehensive explanation of the material on the heat equation presented in the book "An Introduction to Semilinear Evolution Equations" by Cazenave, Haraux, and Martel. The exposition was prepared for the course Modelos de Evolución.

Seminario Avanzado de Matemáticas I

Universidad de Chile

  • This document provides a review of the Krein–Rutman Theorem, including its proof and a typical application to positive eigenfunctions of elliptic operators. The presentation follows Paul H. Rabinowitz, Analyse numérique: Théorie du degré topologique et applications (Gauthier-Villars, 1970), Chapter VIII.3. This work was prepared during the course Seminario Avanzado de Matemáticas I (focused on Nonlinear Analysis).
  • This document presents a detailed review of the paper "A Liouville-type theorem for elliptic systems" by Patricio Felmer and Djairo Guedes de Figueiredo (1994). The analysis focuses on the main result and on the technics involved (Moving planes). This work was prepared during the course Seminario Avanzado de Matemáticas I (focused on Nonlinear Analysis).
  • This document provides a detailed review of the paper "The proof of the Lane–Emden conjecture in 4 space dimensions" by Philippe Souplet (2009). In this document, we aim to provide a detailed exposition of Souplet's proof of the Lane–Emden system conjecture up to dimension N=4. This work was prepared during the course Seminario Avanzado de Matemáticas I (focused on Nonlinear Analysis).
