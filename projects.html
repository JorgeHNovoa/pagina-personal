Predictive Models in Time Series

RialStat SPA.

During the internship at RialStat in the Data Science area, the main tasks involved validating databases from official sources and developing statistical and macroeconomic forecasting models. Using Excel, R (via RStudio and Google Colab), and time series analysis techniques, predictive models such as ARIMA and PROPHET were implemented, showing good performance through backtesting. Overall, the experience provided a solid introduction to data science, particularly in predictive modeling, while also highlighting some academic preparation limitations.

Portfolio Creation using Machine Learning

RialStat SPA

This internship focused on constructing investment portfolios using mathematical statistical models and Artificial Intelligence. The methodology involved studying transformer-based Long-Time Series prediction techniques from research papers and implementing these models to predict asset values within specific time windows. The portfolio creation process included studying general portfolio theory and optimization methods for asset selection under given constraints (maximizing returns, minimizing risk). While the transformer model predictions showed promising results for asset forecasting, limitations such as insufficient variables and lack of exogenous factors prevented conclusive findings. The experience provided valuable introduction to data science and portfolio management, deepening knowledge of predictive models for financial assets.
