DIM's Master Seminar

Departamento de Ingeniería Matemática — Universidad de Chile

In this seminar, the master's students of the Department of Mathematical Engineering present the main problem and/or research question they work on in their thesis, using a low-technical-level tone that makes each session accessible to everyone in the academic community of the department.

The main goal is to share with the community the research fields being developed in the DIM's master program, strengthening awareness of the state of the art and the many possibilities of pure or applied mathematical research in the department.

(Almost) every session is in Spanish. Recordings are available on the YouTube playlist.
